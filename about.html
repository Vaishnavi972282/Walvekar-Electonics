About Walvekar Electronics

Serving the community with quality electronic products since 1960

Our Story

Founded in 1960, Walvekar Electronics started as a small shop with a big dream - to provide quality electronic products to our community. Over the years, we have grown from a single store to two branches, but our commitment to customer satisfaction remains unchanged.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to make quality electronics accessible to everyone. Today, we continue to uphold this mission while adapting to the changing needs of our customers.
